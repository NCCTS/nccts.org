We pray you will join us in the work of a lifetime.

The National Catholic Conference for Total Stewardship (NCCTS) was founded by the late Fr. Francis A. Novak, C.Ss.R., and is committed to spreading his vision for a great renewal in Catholic parish life throughout the world.

The CLCC Formation Process sprang from his heart as a gift to the Church he so loved. Father believed it to be the work of God the Holy Spirit in him, for the benefit of his brothers and sisters in Christ.

We invite you to learn more about CLCC, and to begin the process in your homes and parishes.
