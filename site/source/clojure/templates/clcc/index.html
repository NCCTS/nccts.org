CLCC

Celebrating Life as a Catholic Christian (CLCC) is a process for New Evangelization that develops knowledge of the faith in Catholics starving for Gospel truth. It is modeled from official Church documents of our modern popes with a view to ignite a passion to grow in personal holiness and zeal to help carry out a high priority of the Church's mission: to receover millions of lapsed, indifferent, fallen-away Catholics by personal outreach to them.
